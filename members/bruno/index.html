Bruno LEME

Postdoctoral Associate, University of Florida

Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate and responsible for the (Phenomics Robotics) Group in the Blueberry breeding and Genomics Laboratory at University of Florida. The group study manners of implementing engineering solutions for acquiring multimodal phenotypic data in crops which can be used for facilitating plant breeding. In addition, I am a Visiting Researcher in the Industrial Cyber-Physical Systems Research Center at the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST) in Tokyo, Japan.

In the subject of my academic achievements, I have a Ph.D. degree in Robotics from the Systems and Information Engineering School at University of Tsukuba, Japan. Moreover, I have a Master’s degree with focus on Automation from the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Taubate, Brazil.
